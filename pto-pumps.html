PTO Pumps

Made in Italy, Vincenzi type VG tractor driven pumps, with integral step up gearbox to suit PTO drives.

Ideal for irrigation applications where portability and flexibility is required.

Pump units are available in single stage for standard applications, and two stage designs for higher pressures.

VG brochure
Vincenzi PTO pumps feature an overdrive unit with helical cut, nickel-chromium, carburised and hardened alloy steel gears in an oil bath for maximum life and reliability
Vincenzi single stage pumps are capable of flows up to 3,000 L/min, and heads of up to 120m. Simple and robust design and construction proven over decades.
Vincenzi Two stage pumps are designed for efficient pumping at high pressures. These pumps are capable of flows up to 2,800 L/min and heads of up to 180m
Vincenzi 2-wheel site carts are available from stock. These have an anti-tipping bar out the front, and adjustable length tow-hitch. They also feature a cradle to carry the PTO drive shaft when not in use.
3-point linkage frames are available to suit various sized Tractor 3-point link systems. Frames can be supplied with fixed or adjustable feet, and with optional hand priming pump
